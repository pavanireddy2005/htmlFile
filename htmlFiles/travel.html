Travel destinations in Agra

Agra is a major city in the state of Uttar Pradesh. Agra was the capital of the Mughal Empire in India. It is located 200 km south of Delhi, on the banks of the Yamuna. It is home to many historical monuments from the Mughal era.

Popular destinations

  • Taj Mahal
  • Agra Fort
  • Ram Bagh

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is one of the most famous landmarks in India and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Agra Fort

Agra Fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of Mughal architecture. It was the main residence of the Mughal emperors until 1638.

Ram Bagh

Ram Bagh is the oldest Mughal garden in India, built by Babur, the first Mughal emperor. It is a beautiful garden with fountains and pathways.
